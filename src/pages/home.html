What is Raimu?

Raimu is a browser-based video chat platform that brings a fresh twist to online communication. Like its namesake - the Japanese word for lime - it's refreshingly simple, yet full of zest.

No downloads, no accounts, no configuration needed. Just create a room, share the link, and start talking in crystal-clear quality.
